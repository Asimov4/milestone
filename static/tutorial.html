How to Create an Account on LinkedIn

LinkedIn is a social networking site used specifically for professional purposes. You can use it to connect yourself to your existing professional network as well as expand your network to your secondary and tertiary connections through those you already know.

Creating an account and getting started is easy, just follow the steps below:

  • Step Number 1

    Go to www.linkedin.com

    Sign up by entering you name, email and a password in the Get started it's free box on the right of the screen. Then click the Join Now button.



  • Step Number 2

    Creating Your Professional Profile

    It is quick and easy. Enter the information of your country, postal code, what you're doing currently (employed/job seeker/student), your school/university and the dates you attend it. Then click the Create my profile button



  • Step Number 3

    Grow Your Network

    This part is optional but it is highly encouraged you get through this part to make it easier for you to be connected with your already list of friends in LinkedIn

    Go add your email address again and then click the Continue button



  • Step Number 4

    Accept LinkedIn Request

    LinkedIn will be requesting your consent to view your email address and manage your contacts. Just click the Accept button



  • Step Number 5

    Connect With People You Know

    These are the list of your friends on LinkedIn already. You can select the people that you would like to connect by clicking them individually or click select all which are set automatically when you get on this page. Then click Add Connections button



  • Step Number 6

    Invite More People

    This part is optional. These are the list of your friend's email that have not joined LinkedIn and you can invite them to open a LinkedIn account. The more the merrier, right!

    Continue by clicking Add to network button or simply click Skip this step.



  • Step Number 7

    Do You Know These People

    You can add these people and then click the Add Connection(s) or simply click Skip this step



  • Step Number 8

    Share The News

    You've just completed your professional profile. Share the news on Facebook or Twitter or click Skip this step



  • Step Number 9

    Edit Your Profile

  • Step Number 10

    Confirm Your Account Through Your Email Address

    Go check your email and confirm your email address.
